# cleanup-standard.md (shared)

Strip AI-tool scaffolding and turn prompt output into prose a client/investor/donor can
read. (Superset of Nina `/polish` and the Madison-Pitch Clean-Up Standard.) Apply in
order. This is a finalization step — the substance stays; the presentation changes.

## Rule 1 — Strip scaffolding
Remove every internal label: slide/step numbers ("Slide 2 —", "STEP 1 —", "PART A —"),
framework section names ("THE HOOK", "Voice Pillars", "JARGON AUDIT", "SPOKEN SCRIPT"),
prompt artifacts ("Task:", "Format:", "Output as:"), rating labels (🔴/🟡/🟢), and
meta-instructions ("under 100 words", "be specific", word-count notations "(87 words)").

## Rule 2 — SAS titles (Standalone Sentence headers)
Every remaining header is a full sentence: subject + active verb + specific claim, under
14 words, no buzzwords. Reject label heads ("The Problem"), gerund heads ("Building the
Identity"), vague nouns ("Next Steps").
- ✗ "Competitive Landscape" → ✓ "No competitor in this space owns warmth and precision together"
- ✗ "The Ask" → ✓ "We are seeking three partners with a story no one has heard"

## Rule 3 — New Yorker pacing
1. Sentence variety: follow every sentence over 25 words with one under 10.
2. Light openers: begin some sentences with "But," "Yet," "And" for rhythm — not always.
3. No more than two statistics in a row without a narrative sentence between them.
4. One grounding human detail per section (a name, a place, a number that means something).
5. Each section ends on its shortest sentence — the one the reader remembers.

## Rule 4 — Destination language
Apply `destination-language.md`: replace process/engine terms with outcome/destination
terms; remove hedging and self-congratulation.

## Rule 5 — Deliver
1. **CLEAN VERSION** — the full document rewritten for the reader; reads as professional
   prose, not prompt output.
2. **CHANGE LOG** (internal only) — two columns: original label/term | action taken.
3. **READINESS SIGNAL** — one sentence: client-ready, or flag what still needs human
   review (unverified statistics, placeholder brackets, sections marked TBD).
