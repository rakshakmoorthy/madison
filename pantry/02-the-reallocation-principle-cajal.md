_Density: recommend 4 figures, Mixed._

This is the densest chapter so far and carries four strong figure candidates, three of them already marked in the manuscript. First, the five work types (execution, evidence, judgment, approval, learning) form a linear-cyclical flow with a feedback loop — a systems/cycle diagram and the chapter's spine. The reinvestment-hypothesis sentence structure and the reallocation-plan table are prose/template material that the time-allocation chart and the flow already cover conceptually, so they are folded in rather than given separate figures.
